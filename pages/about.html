Our Story

Design is Not Decoration.
It is Emotion.

The Founders

Julia & Alfredo

JCL Staging was born from a simple conviction: the way a home feels is more powerful than how it looks.

Julia Carias-Linares, Creative Director, brings a painter's eye to every project. Alfredo Linares, Lead Executor, ensures vision becomes flawless reality. Together since 2017, they have transformed over 850 properties across the Tri-State area.

Emotion First

We design for feeling before function. A buyer must fall in love the moment they cross the threshold — before they read a floor plan or hear a price. Emotion closes deals that logic cannot.

Meticulous Craft

No detail is beneath our attention. The angle of a throw pillow, the scent of a room, the precise height of an art piece — these are the invisible touches that separate good staging from unforgettable staging.

Genuine Partnership

We are not vendors — we are collaborators. Many of our clients return project after project, trusting us not just with their properties but with their reputations. Long-term partnership is the foundation of everything we do.

Our Journey

A Decade of Transformation

2017

JCL Staging & Design founded by Julia Carias-Linares and Alfredo Linares in New York City, with a single mission: to bring emotional intelligence to luxury real estate presentation.

2018

First luxury listing sold in 3 days, 22% above asking price — setting the tone for what every JCL project could achieve.

2019

Strategic partnerships established with Douglas Elliman, Compass, and Sotheby's International Realty — becoming the preferred staging studio for New York's most prestigious brokerages.

2020

Expanded service territory to include New Jersey and Connecticut, growing the team and inventory to meet increasing demand across the full Tri-State region.

2021

500th property staged — a milestone celebrated with the launch of our expanded luxury inventory and a new client experience program built around white-glove service.

2022

Launched the Builder & Developer division, offering full-scale model home programs and pre-construction staging services to residential developers across the region.

2023

Named Top Staging Studio by NY Real Estate Magazine — recognized for innovation, client results, and setting a new standard for luxury staging in the metropolitan area.

2024

Launched Olivia, our proprietary AI Design Concierge — combining the warmth of human expertise with the intelligence of artificial intelligence to guide clients through every decision.

2025

850+ properties transformed, 97% client satisfaction rate, and a team of dedicated designers, stylists, and project managers committed to one standard: extraordinary.
