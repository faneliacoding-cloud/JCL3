Selling Your Home

The Case for Staging

Staged homes spend an average of 73% less time on the market than unstaged properties. But the deeper truth is emotional: buyers who feel a connection to a space act quickly and decisively. We design that feeling deliberately — using light, material, and proportion to create an experience that buyers cannot shake. Our average property sells within 8 days of listing.
Industry data consistently shows that staging returns $5–$10 for every $1 invested. For luxury properties in the Tri-State area, that return is often significantly higher. Several of our recent listings achieved 15–25% above asking price — a direct result of the emotional impact of exceptional staging.
Always before. The first 7 days of a listing generate the most buyer interest, the most showings, and the most competitive offers. We coordinate with your timeline to ensure your home is staged, photographed, and market-ready before the listing goes live.
Especially in a buyer's market. When inventory is high, staging becomes the single most powerful differentiator. Well-staged homes not only show better in person — they photograph dramatically better, attracting significantly more online views, which translates into more showings and faster sales.

Investment and Pricing

Transparent, Tailored Pricing

Staging investment is tailored to each property — based on square footage, the number of rooms being staged, the staging duration (typically tied to listing period), and the service level. We provide detailed, transparent proposals following our initial consultation, with no hidden costs or surprise fees.
Our comprehensive packages include: initial property consultation and design concept development; furniture, art, and accessory selection from our curated inventory; professional delivery, installation, and styling; finishing and pre-photography walkthrough; ongoing support during the listing period; and coordinated removal after closing.
We offer flexible payment arrangements for qualified clients, including programs where staging fees are collected at closing — meaning no out-of-pocket cost during the listing period. This is discussed and arranged during your consultation.
