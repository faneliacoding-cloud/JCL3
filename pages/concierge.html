About Olivia

Olivia is an AI assistant trained comprehensively on JCL Staging & Design's portfolio, methodology, pricing, and processes. She uses advanced natural language understanding to provide personalized guidance on staging, interior design, and the luxury real estate market. Olivia is not a generic chatbot — she is a curated intelligence built around JCL's specific expertise and aesthetic philosophy.

Your privacy is paramount to us. All conversations with Olivia are encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not share your personal information with third parties, and your chat history is used only to improve your experience within your session. You can request deletion of your conversation data at any time by contacting us at privacy@jclstaging.com.
